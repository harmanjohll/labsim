SciSim Games / Happy Elements

Happy Family: Periodic Table Edition

Collect all 4 elements of the same group to form a family!

How to Play

  • On your turn, ask any player for a specific element card
  • If they have it, you get the card and go again
  • If not, they say "Not at home!" and the turn passes
  • Complete a family by collecting all 4 elements from one group
  • The player with the most families wins!
